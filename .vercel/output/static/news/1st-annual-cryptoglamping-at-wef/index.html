CryptoGlamping at WEF 2025: The Ultimate Networking Retreat

Are you ready to redefine your World Economic Forum (WEF) experience? The 1st Annual CryptoGlamping event, running from January 20–24, 2025, offers a unique opportunity to stay in the same luxurious VIP motor coaches once used by global icons like Adele, Ed Sheeran, and Lady Gaga during their European tours.

Hosted at RinerLodge, Davos, CryptoGlamping is designed for the crypto community, offering a perfect balance of networking, relaxation, and exclusivity. Located just 10 minutes from Davos Platz, you'll be at the heart of the action while enjoying unparalleled comfort.

Highlights:

  • Luxury Accommodations: Enjoy cozy and stylish accommodations with premium amenities, including a private lounge, galley, and sleeping quarters.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow crypto enthusiasts, from unicorns to whales, in a vibrant and tailored community.
  • Recreational Bliss: Unwind with built-in entertainment systems, including PlayStation, after a long day of events.
  • Prime Location: Ski slopes, PostBus stops, and train stations are just steps away.

Availability is limited, and only full motor coach rentals (up to 16 guests per coach) are offered. Secure your spot today and immerse yourself in a rock-star lifestyle while advancing your crypto ambitions.
